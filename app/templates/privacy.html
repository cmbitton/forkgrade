Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

ForkGrade ("we", "us", or "our") operates forkgrade.com. This page explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use ForkGrade. We collect standard server log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring URL) for operational purposes. This data is not sold or shared with third parties.

Cookies

We use cookies for site functionality and advertising. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work correctly.

Advertising

ForkGrade uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners use cookies (including the DoubleClick cookie) to serve ads based on your prior visits to this site and other sites on the internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings or aboutads.info.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to ForkGrade and other sites. For more information on how Google uses data when you use our site, visit google.com/policies/privacy/partners.

Third-Party Links

ForkGrade links to government data sources and may display ads linking to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Data Sources

All health inspection records displayed on ForkGrade are sourced from public government databases. We do not collect or store any personal information about restaurant owners, employees, or members of the public from these records.

Children's Privacy

ForkGrade is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
